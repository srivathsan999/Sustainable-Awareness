Our story

Designing services that uplift people and the planet

Sustainable Awareness unites strategists, designers, technologists, and scientists to help service-based businesses reimagine impact.

Mission

Empower service teams with intelligence, playbooks, and creativity to decarbonize operations without slowing growth.

Vision

Become the go-to studio for eco-forward services, where clients deliver measurable social and environmental value.

Values
  • Stewardship

Our history

Milestones that shaped Sustainable Awareness

2013 · Founded in Portland

Started as a boutique consultancy helping local service companies measure emissions.

2017 · Launched analytics lab

Built proprietary dashboards connecting operational data with ESG reporting.

2021 · Expanded globally

Opened satellite studios in Berlin and Dubai to support multilingual teams.

2024 · Productized playbooks

Introduced Sustainable Awareness SaaS to help clients scale repeatable workflows.
